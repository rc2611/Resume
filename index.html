About Me

I am a PhD candidate at the Indian Institute of Technology Delhi (IIT Delhi), specializing in Water Resources Engineering. My research focuses on atmospheric moisture transport and its role in hydrological extremes. I hold a Bachelor's degree in Civil Engineering from the National Institute of Technology Hamirpur (NIT Hamirpur) and a Master of Science by Research from the Indian Institute of Technology Palakkad (IIT Palakkad).

I am currently engaged in a project that explores the fascinating relationship between atmospheric moisture transport and hydrological extremes, particularly floods. By analyzing historical data, conducting advanced modeling, and examining case studies, I aim to uncover the intricate mechanisms driving these phenomena.

Research Interests

My research interests include:

  • Atmospheric Moisture Transport
  • Hydrological Extremes
  • Water Resources Management
  • Hydrological Modeling
